About Me

I am a dedicated Mathematics Professor with a strong passion for teaching, research, and mathematical exploration. My expertise spans Algebra, Geometry, and Applied Mathematics, where I have made significant contributions through rigorous academic inquiry and innovative problem-solving approaches.
